Kozhikode

Calicut, now known as Kozhikode is the largest settlement in the North Kerala. Having been the epicentre of trade for many centuries it was also the capital of the Zamorin dynasty. The place is famous for being the arrival point of Vasco Da Gama on the Indian Subcontinent. The place has witnessed Portuguese and Dutch presence after the Arab traders and the influence on the local landscape and culture is omnipresent.

Hot tourist spots in Kozhikode

Thikkoti Light House

Said to be built on the remains of a shipwreck, the Thikkoti Lighthouse is one of the major attractions of Kozhikode. Located on a rocky shoreline, the place is best known for the scenic views it offers several species of nomadic birds.

Thusharagiri Waterfall

Lying in the Western Ghats of Kozhikode district in Kerala, Thusharagiri Fall cascades down as three waterfalls. Located in a small tribal village, the name of the falls means snow covered mountains'.
